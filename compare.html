FAQ

Why don't you offer company credit scores?
Company credit scores are commoditised. What the others miss is director risk. A company can look healthy on paper while its director has left a trail of liquidated businesses and unpaid debts. We score the person, not just the entity.
Can I use Director Intel alongside Creditsafe?
Yes. Many customers use us as a second layer. Your existing provider gives you the company view; we give you the director view.
What if I want to cancel?
Log in, click cancel, done. No phone calls, no retention teams.
Will my price increase at renewal?
No. If you sign up at £99/month, you stay at £99/month.
How fresh is your data?
Real-time. Every search pulls directly from Companies House, The Gazette, the FCA Register, the Insolvency Register, and the UK Sanctions List.
