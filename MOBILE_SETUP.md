## How to Listen on Your Phone

### Method 1: iCloud Drive (iPhone/iPad)

1. **On your Mac:**
   - Go to `/Users/jaymychalkiw/Desktop/padsonpads/`
   - Select these files:
     - `audiobook.html`
     - `audio` folder (with all 8 MP3 files inside)
   - Drag them to **iCloud Drive**

2. **On your iPhone/iPad:**
   - Open **Files** app
   - Go to **iCloud Drive**
   - Find the `audiobook.html` file
   - Tap it - it will open in Safari
   - Tap a chapter and press play!
